Vigenère Cipher Encoder & Decoder

The Vigenère Cipher is a method of encrypting alphabetic text using a series of Caesar Ciphers based on the letters of a keyword. Both encryption and decryption rely on the keyword.

How It Works:

  • Each letter in the plaintext is shifted by the position of the corresponding letter in the keyword.
  • The keyword repeats when it is shorter than the text.
